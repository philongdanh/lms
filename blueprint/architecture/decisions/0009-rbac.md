# ADR-0009: RBAC with 5 Seed Roles

Cần authorization model linh hoạt.

---

## Decision

**RBAC** với 5 roles: `root-admin`, `tenant-admin`, `teacher`, `parent`,
`student`.

---

## Rationale

Permission-based, hierarchy rõ ràng, dễ mở rộng.
